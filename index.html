resQroute

The Dynamic Traffic Light Management System uses real-time **GPS tracking** to detect approaching ambulances and automatically **adjust traffic signals**. When an ambulance is **within 1 km** of an intersection, the system turns the traffic light **green in its path** and **red for other vehicles**, ensuring a clear route. Once the ambulance **crosses the junction**, signals return to normal. This **AI-powered, IoT-based system** reduces delays, improves emergency response times, and minimizes traffic disruptions. 🚑🚦
